Sweet Treats Bakery - Site Plan

Site Name

Sweet Treats Bakery

This name was chosen because it clearly conveys our focus on delicious baked goods while creating a welcoming and approachable vibe for customers.

Site Purpose

The Sweet Treats Bakery website will serve as a digital storefront to:

  • Showcase our daily menu and signature items
  • Enable online ordering for pickup/delivery
  • Provide location information and business hours
  • Share our baking philosophy and quality ingredients

Scenarios

  • What gluten-free options are available this week?
  • How far in advance should I order a custom birthday cake?
  • Do you offer vegan or dairy-free alternatives?

Color Schema

Primary (#FFB6C1)
Secondary (#FFF5E1)
Accent (#8B4513)

Usage:

  • Primary: Headers and key accents
  • Secondary: Background colors
  • Accent: Headings and important buttons

Typography

Playfair Display - Used for headings and titles

Open Sans - Used for body text and paragraphs
